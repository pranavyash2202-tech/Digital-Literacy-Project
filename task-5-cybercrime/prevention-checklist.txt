Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they
claim to be from a bank. Always verify unknown calls, emails, or
messages before taking any action. Do not click on suspicious links or
download attachments from unknown sources. Use strong and unique
passwords for different accounts and update them regularly. Enable
two-factor authentication (2FA) for email, banking, and social media
accounts. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts
or making transactions. Keep your phone and apps updated to protect
against security vulnerabilities. Log out from devices after use,
especially on shared or public systems.

Financial / UPI Safety Tips:

Remember: Entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money, not
receiving it. Never accept collect/payment requests from unknown persons
or unverified sources.
