Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

Do’s: - Think before posting; ensure your content is respectful and
appropriate. - Maintain privacy by adjusting account settings and
limiting personal information. - Use professional language, especially
in academic or work-related contexts. - Verify information before
sharing to avoid misinformation. - Be positive and constructive in
comments and discussions.

Don’ts: - Do not share sensitive personal information like address,
phone number, or passwords. - Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or
inappropriate content. - Do not engage in online arguments or
cyberbullying. - Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information. -
Do not post content that can harm your reputation or future career.
